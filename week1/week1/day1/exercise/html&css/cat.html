About domestic cats

They often called house cats when kept as indoor pets or simply cats when there is no need to distinguish them from other fileds and felines. They are often valued by humans for companionship

There are more than seventy cat breeds recognized by various cat registries

Cat Senses

  • They can hear sound too faint or too high in frequency for human ears
  • They can see in near darkness
  • They have poorer color vision and a better sense of smell than human

The lions

Present inAfrica and India, the lion typically inhabits grasslands and savannahs, but is absent in dense forests. It usually more than other big cats, but when persecuted adapts to being active at night and at twilight. A lion pride consists of few adult males, related females and cubs. Prides vary in seize and composition from three to 20 adult lion, depending on habitat and prey availability. Females cooperate when hunting and prey mostly on large ungulates, including antelops, deer, buffalo, zebra and even giraffe.
