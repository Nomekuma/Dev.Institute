Rishta Nata Department of Ahmadiyya Muslim Jama'at Canada
 

A community portal for Rishta Nata

 

This website is designed to facilitate the Rishta Nata department of Ahmadiyya Muslim Community. Though this website welcomes all members of Ahmadiyya Muslim Community around the globe, it still has a specific focus for serving Canadian members of the community among all of our users.

 

If you do not belong to Ahmadiyya Muslim Community, please note that this is not a public website and has a specific target audience. This website only intends to serve members of Ahmadiyya Muslim Community for the specific cause of Rishta Nata. If you need any information about Ahmadiyya Muslim Community, you can visit our website at alislam.org or our Canadian local site at ahmadiyya.ca

 

Members of Ahmadiyya Muslim Community may submit their particulars/profile through this website which in turn will be used by the officials in Rishta Nata Department Canada to find a suitable match. If you want to submit your profile to the system please click here to Singup online.
